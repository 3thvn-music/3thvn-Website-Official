About 3THVN

3THVN is more than an artist name—it's a movement. With cinematic textures, deep basslines, and emotional melodies, 3THVN crafts stories that resonate through sound. From humble beginnings to a growing fanbase, the journey reflects persistence, passion, and purpose.

Whether you're listening under city lights or stars above, the music aims to connect hearts across the globe. Every beat is a piece of 3THVN's soul, made to echo in yours.
